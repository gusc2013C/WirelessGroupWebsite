Principal Investigator

Xi Tian (田曦)

Assistant Professor
Institute of Data and Information, Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School

Bio: Xi Tian is an Assistant Professor in the Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School (SIGS) at Tsinghua University. He received the B.S. degree in electrical engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2016, and the Ph.D. degree in microwave and radio frequency from the National University of Singapore (NUS) in 2021, and conducted his postdoctoral studies at the Institute for Health Innovation and Technology (iHealthtech), NUS. His research focuses on the development of advanced wireless technologies to address important scientific and biomedical challenges in human health.
Email: tianxi@sz.tsinghua.edu.cn
